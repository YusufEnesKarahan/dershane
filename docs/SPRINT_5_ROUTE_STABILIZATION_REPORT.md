## 7. Production risk değerlendirmesi

Route and integration risk is **low** after this sprint: there are no duplicate route names, no registered route points to a missing controller method, and static route references are resolved. The residual risk is limited to environment-level Composer access and browser-level role/permission scenarios that require real authenticated users.
