# Asset Management Module

The Asset Management module tracks hardware and physical assets within the classroom network:

- **Asset Categories (`asset_categories`)**: Group structures like PCs, furniture, projectors, air conditioners.
- **Assets (`assets`)**: Individual asset registers storing barcode serials, purchase logs, locations, and condition states (`active`, `maintenance`, `broken`, `retired`).
- **Asset Locations (`asset_locations`)**: Physical rooms or zones tied to specific branches.
- **Assignments (`asset_assignments`)**: Records detailing active device handovers to employee staff and return histories.
