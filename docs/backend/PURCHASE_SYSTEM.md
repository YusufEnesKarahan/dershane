# Purchase Order Management

The Purchase Order system facilitates procurement transactions with third-party vendors:

- **Suppliers (`suppliers`)**: Contact details, tax registrations, and order volumes.
- **Purchase Orders (`purchase_orders`)**: Outlines requisitions, total amounts, date registers, and statuses (`pending`, `approved`, `completed`, `cancelled`).
- **Maintenance Records (`maintenance_records`)**: Keeps track of assets undergoing repair and diagnostic logging.
